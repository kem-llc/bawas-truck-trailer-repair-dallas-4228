Our story

Dallas' Trusted Heavy-Duty Towing & Repair Experts

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and transparent towing and repair services for commercial vehicles in Dallas, keeping your fleet moving and your business on schedule.

Bawa's Truck & Trailer Repair was founded with a simple goal: provide honest, high-quality heavy-duty towing and repair services to the Dallas community. With decades of combined experience in the trucking industry, our team understands the urgency of getting back on the road. We built our company on a foundation of integrity, reliability, and customer-first service.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of heavy-duty tow trucks and mobile service vehicles, serving trucking companies, construction firms, and independent owner-operators across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. Our 5.0-star rating reflects our commitment to excellence — we treat every vehicle as if it were our own.
